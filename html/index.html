About Us

Our goal is to make it easy for people in your neighborhood to report and fix problems. We want to help communities work together by providing a simple way to share concerns and find solutions. We believe that by making it easier for neighbors to talk and help each other, we can make your community a better place to live. Join us in this mission to build stronger and more connected neighborhoods.

We started because we saw that in many communities, it's hard to report problems, and sometimes it feels like nobody is listening. We wanted to change that. We know how frustrating it can be when things go wrong and no one seems to care.
